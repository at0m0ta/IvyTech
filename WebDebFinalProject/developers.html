"What type of developer should I become?"

Web & Mobile

A Web Developer  is Someone who specializes in building applications that are used in a browser. The languages and software you will be using may be different, but the result is the same. There is a strong focus on User Interaction (UI) and User Experience (UX) in web development. You may also need to work in the backend; This means setting up databases or writing APIs (application programming interface).

A Mobile Developer — Someone who specializes in building applications that are used on mobile devices, such as smartphones or tablets. A strong focus on UI/UX may or may not be needed. Technologies can take two distinct paths, either native or hybrid/cross-platform development. May also require a backend of some sort. They actually both overlap now days. So going into web development will someways assume mobile as well.

Analytics & Data Science

Data Science is a field that is made up of everything that related to data cleansing, preparation, and analysis. Data Science is the combination of statistics, mathematics, programming, problem-solving, capturing data in clever ways, the ability to look at things differently, as well as cleansing, preparing and aligning the data to what is needed. Data Science is basically the general techniques used when trying to extract information from data.

Data Analytics the science of examining the raw data and drawing conclusions about that information. Data Analytics involves applying an algorithms or mechanical process to gain useful insights. For example, running through data sets to look for meaningful correlations.

Data Science is used in a number of industries to allow the organizations to make better decisions as well as verify and disprove existing theories or models. The focus of Data Analytics is inference, the process of deriving conclusions that are solely based on what the researcher already knows.

Cyber Security

Cybersecurity is the protection of internet-connected systems, including hardware, software and data, from cyberattacks. In a computing, security is comprised of cybersecurity and physical security -- both are used by enterprises to protect against unauthorized access to data centers and other systems.

Information security, is designed to maintain the confidentiality, integrity and availability of data, is a subset of cybersecurity.

Analysts in the projected of cybersecurity jobs states that the field will grow 28 percent from 2016 to 2026, faster than the average for other career fields. These analysts will be needed to create solutions to prevent hackers from stealing critical information or causing problems for computer networks.
